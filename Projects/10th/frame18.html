India




India is a vibrant and diverse country in South Asia. It got its freedom on the 15th of August in 1947 from Britain immediately after its partition from its neighbouring Pakistan. Currently it has 29 states, 6 Union States and Delhi - the National Capital Region (NCR) - the majority of which are based on linguistic differences. According to the latest United Nations estimates, the population is around 1,359,278,293 - 17.74% of the world's population. It has a GDP of 2,848,000,000,000 USD (estimate: 2018)
