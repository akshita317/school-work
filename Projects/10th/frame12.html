SPORTS

The School has taken special care to develop the sports facilities. We at JSPS believe that a healthy mind in a sound body leads to excellent all round development. The sports facilities cater to individual games, as well as team game thus nurturing a keen competitive spirit so essential for achieving excellence.





At JSPS, we provide facilities for indoor games like Table Tennis and Shuttle Badminton. Outdoor games like Cricket, Lawn Tennis, Football, Basketball and a good Athletic track are also provided.
