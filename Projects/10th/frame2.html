Admissions




Admission Guidelines
  • Please fill the Application Form for registration.
  • Please submit the Application Form with a copy of your child's birth certificate, 2 passport-sized photographs and a copy of the applicant's previous report cards.
  • Aadhar card of parents and applicant needs to be submitted.
  • Parents will be intimated about the entrance tests and interviews.
  • Please ensure prompt arrival at the venue on the scheduled date of testing or interview.
