Explore Jnana Sweekar

Our rich heritage, ethos and vision give Jnana Sweekar its unique character, which places pupils at the heart of our endeavours.

A centre of academic, sporting and creative excellence in our region and beyond, we inspire pupils to be the best that they can be.





These attributes are reinforced through our world-class curriculum, our rich co-curricular programme and our superb care across the Junior and Senior Schools.





History

JSPS was founded in 2002. We’ve emerged as a leading school offering Indian and international curricula to students from diverse backgrounds.



Since its inception, JSPS has steadfastly focused on providing an extraordinary education to students. In adopting the cultural ideology of “Schools That Learn,” JSPS makes each and every member of our community an equal partner in the process of learning.





Guiding Priciples

At JSPS, we have an ambitious vision: to become a role model for teaching and learning. We nurture our students to be leaders who are committed to excellence, are honest and adaptable and who have a constant ambience for learning. Our high quality education which is imparted by globally experienced and caring teachers build strong values and virtues which aid our students to develop an all rounded personality.





Management

JSPS management is a strong and dynamic team of experienced and knowledgeable individuals who work very hard to fulfil our Vision. It is their constant guidance that has led us to continually achieve milestones for our success.





Achievements

JSPS has been honoured with several recognitions for achieving excellence in school education. Recognised for imparting education using benchmarked quality practices, JSPS is committed to a well-rounded and holistic approach to nurturing young minds for a better tomorrow.
